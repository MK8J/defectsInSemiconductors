Trapping time constants¶

This module allows for simple calculation of trapping time constants.

Parameters:
  • Ed – Energy level of the defect from the intrinsic level
  • sigma_e – The capture cross section of electrons

  • ni – The intrinsic carrier density
  • ne0 – The concentration of electrons in the dark
  • nh0 – The concentration of holes in the dark
  • temp – The temperature of the material
  • tau_bkg – The effective recombination lifetime of the semiconductor
defects.timeConstants.escape_time_constants(Ed, sigma_e, sigma_h, ni, temp, ne0, nh0, bk_tau)[source]¶

returns the escape time constants for a single level defect. These values are returned in a dictionary

Parameters:
  • Ed – activation energy level
  • sigma_e – capture cross section of electrons
  • sigma_h – capture cross section of holes
  • ni – intrinsic carrier density
  • temp – temperature
  • ne0 – number of electrons in the dark
  • nh0 – number of holes in the dark
  • bk_tau – a background lifetime
Returns:

The keys of which are
  • DLTS: the time constant expected from DLTS
  • DLTS-Mo: The time constant expected from minority carrier DLTS
  • PC: the time constant expected from transient photoconductance
  • HH-simp: the time constant expected from transient photoconductance according to the simplified hornbeck and hanes model.
Return type:

dictionary
